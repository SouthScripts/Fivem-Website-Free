About Our Server

What makes Worthless Roleplay different?

Worthless Roleplay is a premium FiveM roleplay server dedicated to providing the most immersive and enjoyable RP experience. Our server features custom scripts, vehicles, and a dedicated team of staff members to ensure a smooth gameplay experience.

Whether you're looking to join the police force, become a criminal mastermind, or live a simple civilian life, our server offers endless possibilities for roleplay scenarios.

Server Rules

Please follow these guidelines for a better experience

1. Respect All Players

Treat all players with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and toxicity will not be tolerated.

2. No Metagaming

Using information obtained outside of roleplay to gain an advantage in-game is prohibited.

3. No Powergaming

Forcing scenarios on other players or performing unrealistic actions is not allowed.

4. Value Your Life

Act as you would in real life when faced with dangerous situations.

5. No RDM/VDM

Random Death Match and Vehicle Death Match are strictly prohibited.

6. Stay In Character

Maintain roleplay at all times in-game and use appropriate channels for OOC communication.

How to Connect

  1. Download and install FiveM
  2. Launch FiveM and click on "Play"
  3. Press F8 to open the console
  4. Type connect play.worthlessrp.com and press Enter
  5. Create your character and start your roleplay journey!
